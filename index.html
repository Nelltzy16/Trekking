Why Trekking in Bukit Lawang?

Bukit Lawang is the gateway to Gunung Leuser National Park, home to the rare Sumatran orangutan and a variety of exotic flora and fauna. Trekking here gives you an incredible experience of walking along natural trails, seeing wildlife, and staying overnight in the middle of the forest.
